# Data

## Source

SOA 2014 Post-Level Term Lapse and Mortality Study
Society of Actuaries / RGA Reinsurance Company

## Download

1. Go to: https://www.soa.org/resources/experience-studies/2014/research-2014-post-level-shock/
2. Download: research-2014-post-level-term-lapse-study.zip
3. Extract contents into this directory (data/)

## Files Expected

- SOA Post-Level Term Lapse and Mortality Study.xlsx
- SOA Post-Level Term Multivariate Lapse Rate Model.xlsx

## Declared Measurement Mapping Constraints

- Aggregated industry experience across multiple companies
- Each row represents a cell: unique combination of study year,
  duration, gender, issue age, face amount, premium jump magnitude
- Individual policy-level relational structure partially discarded
  through aggregation — declared, not assumed absent
- Premium jump bands are ordinal categories per SOA study design
